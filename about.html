Who We Are

Founded in 2010 in Lahore, Pakistan, Nadia's Saloon is a premium beauty destination built on skill, trust, and attention to detail. We offer expert hair styling, colour, facials, bridal makeup, and nail art — all under one roof.

Every service is delivered by trained professionals using trusted professional lines suited to our weather and hair types.

  • Premium Products — Olaplex, Italian dyes, and organic skincare
